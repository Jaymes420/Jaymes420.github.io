A MAN OF MANY NAMES

From 1974 to 1986, he went by many names. The Visalia Ransacker, The East Area Rapist, The Original Night Stalker, The Diamond Knot Killer, and The Golden State Killer, But now, at last, his true identity has been revealed. Joseph James DeAngelo. So who was he? He was a Husband and a father. he was a fisherman and a cop. He was also one of the most profilic serial offenders in american history. it is beleived that Deangelo starting his decade plus long crime spree in Vasalia, California From April 1974 to December 1975, and which time it's beleived that he was responsible for one murder and up to 120 burglaries. He was breaking into houses at an incredibly high rate, on one occasion he broke into 5 homes in just one day.Read More Here

In June 1976, He moved and began targeting Sacromento, California, and would continue to do so until July 1979. It is during this time that he would aquire the moniker "The East Area Rapist".His M.O has now evolved from ransacking empty homes to breaking into the homes of women alone and raping them at gun point. During this two year period he would commit 51 Rapes, and 2 murders, although he began targetting women alone in their homes, he would eventually evolve a preferance of attacking couples.Read More Here

In October 1979, De Angelo moved to southern California, and begin attacking in Santa barbra county. It is during this six year span that he would aquire his next moniker. " The Original Night Stalker". Once again , his M.O evolves once more and he begins murdering the couples he targets. During this time he would attack seven homes, raping the women, and then murdering both the husband and wife. 10 people in total died in these attacks, with only the first couple surviving. After these final attacks, he vanishes like a ghost, leaving a trail of blood and broken families in his wake. His crimes are commited to the pages of crime books and cold case files. That is until April 24, 2018, after new evidence is gathered by Paul Holes and his team during familial D.N.A. The Sacramento County Sheriff's Department arrest 72-year-old Joseph James DeAngelo in connection with the crimes.

There is a lot we know about his heinous 12 year crime spree, but there are still some questions left unanswered. questions like :
  • what compelled him to start killing?
  • How did he choose his Victims?
  • Why did he stop?

There is much speculation regarding the answers to these questions, as well as many others, but De Angelo has offered up no answers himself. He has not spoken about any of his crimes, of it he has it is not being shared through the meida.
